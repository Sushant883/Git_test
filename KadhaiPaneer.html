This is the Recipe of Kadhai Paneer

Kadhai Paneer is a popular and flavorful North Indian dish made with paneer (Indian cottage cheese), bell peppers, and a variety of spices. Here's a step-by-step recipe to make this delicious dish. Kadhai Paneer is a popular Indian dish made with paneer (Indian cottage cheese) cooked in a flavorful blend of spices, onions, tomatoes, and bell peppers. It's known for its smoky flavor, achieved by cooking in a "kadhai" (wok). Typically served with naan or roti, it's a delicious, rich, and aromatic vegetarian dish.

    For the Kadhai Paneer Masala

  • 2 teaspoons coriander seeds
  • 2 dry red chilies
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1 teaspoon black peppercorns
  • 1 small cinnamon stick (optional)

    For the Gravy

  • 200 grams paneer (cubed)
  • 1 large onion (finely chopped)
  • 1 large tomato (finely chopped or pureed)
  • 1 green bell pepper (capsicum), cut into cubes
  • 1 red bell pepper, cut into cubes
  • 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste
